# Introduction

Welcome to the world of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics!

This book is your guide to understanding the exciting field of embodied intelligence, where artificial intelligence meets the physical world. We will explore how humanoid robots perceive their environment, make decisions, and interact with the world around them.

<!-- Placeholder for Diagram: Overview of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics components -->

Throughout this book, we will cover a wide range of topics, including:

-   **The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2):** Learn the fundamentals of the Robot Operating System (ROS), the standard for robotics software development.
-   **The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity):** Discover how to create and use simulations to test and train your robots in a virtual environment.
-   **The AI-Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac):** Dive into the world of AI for robotics with NVIDIA's powerful Isaac SDK.
-   **Vision-Language-Action (VLA):** Explore the cutting-edge of AI, where robots can understand and respond to natural language commands.

By the end of this book, you will have a solid understanding of the principles of Physical AI and the tools and techniques used to build and program humanoid robots.
